Frequently asked questions

What are credits and how do they work?
Credits are used for scanning features: Backlinks Profile Scanner, Google Search Scanner, and Check My Backlinks. Each scan uses credits based on the number of results. Credits refresh monthly on your billing date.
What's the difference between Free and paid marketplace access?
Free users can browse the marketplace but publisher domain names are hidden. Paid plans show all domain names, letting you see exactly which sites sell links and at what prices.
Can I upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time?
Yes! You can change your plan anytime from your account settings. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades apply at the next billing cycle. Cancel with one click—no questions asked.
Do you offer refunds?
We offer a full refund within 7 days of subscribing to any paid plan—no questions asked. Just contact support.
