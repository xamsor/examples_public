Frequently asked questions

What are credits and how do they work?
Credits power the scanning features. Each feature costs a different amount based on data complexity. Credits refresh monthly on your billing date.
Feature Cost
Google Search Scanner 60 credits/scan
Backlinks Profile Scanner 2 credits/domain
Backlinks Monitoring 1 credit/link
Contact Data Extraction 15 credits/domain

Example: With 5,000 Pro credits, you could run 50 Google Search scans + monitor 1,000 backlinks + extract 100 contact emails each month.

How many Google Search scans can I run?
Each Google Search scan costs 60 credits and analyzes up to 200 search results. With Free (120 credits) you get 2 scans/month. With Pro (5,000 credits) you get 83 scans/month. With Business (50,000 credits) you get 833 scans/month.
What does Backlinks Profile Scanner do?
It extracts refdomains (unique linking domains) for any website. This helps you analyze competitors' backlink profiles or research potential link sources. Each domain analyzed costs 2 credits.
How does Backlinks Monitoring work?
We crawl your backlinks to check if they're still live, follow/nofollow, and indexed. Each link checked costs 1 credit per crawl. If you monitor 100 links weekly, that's 400 credits/month.
What is Contact Data Extraction?
Our experimental feature finds email contacts for any domain. Useful for outreach when you've found a good link prospect. Each extraction costs 15 credits per domain.
