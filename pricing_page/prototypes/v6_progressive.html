Frequently asked questions

Will I save enough to justify $29/month?
Average savings: $37 per link. The subscription pays for itself after one link purchase. If you build 50 links/month (typical for active link builders), that's $1,850/month in potential savings for $29. The math is straightforward — even saving on 1 link per month makes it worth it.
What features use credits vs. which are unlimited?
Core features — Price Finder and Marketplace browsing — are unlimited and don't consume credits. Credits are only for advanced scanning tools: Google Search Scanner (60 credits/scan), Backlinks Profile Scanner (2 credits/domain), and Backlinks Monitoring (1 credit/link). Most users never run out of credits.
What is "Preview only" marketplace?
Free users can browse and filter the marketplace, but publisher domain names are hidden. Upgrade to see exactly which sites are selling links and compare their prices across marketplaces.
Can I upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time?
Yes! You can change your plan anytime from your account settings. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades apply at the next billing cycle. Cancel with one click—no questions asked.
Do you offer refunds?
We offer a full refund within 7 days of subscribing to any paid plan—no questions asked. Just contact support.
